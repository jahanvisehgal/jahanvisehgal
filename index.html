J-NEE

Jahanvi Sehgal aka J-NEE, is a 19 year old, Jaipur (Rajasthan) based rapper. She started writing at the age of 15 and began by penning down short quotes and poems. Later, she discovered open mics and gave a try to spoken word poetry, which helped her gain more experience with the mic and stage. In 2018, she found about Indian HipHop scene and listened to FARAK by DIVINE and found it relatable. Since then, she started posting rap videos online on Instagram and created a local audience. She has put out 2 tracks which are available on YouTube and is coming with new tracks real soon.
